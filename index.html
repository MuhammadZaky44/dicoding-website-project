What is covid-19?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

Symptoms of Covid-19

There is many type of covid-19 symptoms that you need to be aware of. Here is some of covid-19 symptoms.

Most common symptoms:

  • fever
  • cough
  • tiredness
  • loss of taste or smell

Less common symptoms:

  • sore throat
  • headache
  • aches and pains
  • diarrhea
  • a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes
  • red or irritated eyes

Serious symptoms:

  • difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • loss of speech or mobility, or confusion
  • chest pain

Seek for medical attention if you have serious symptoms. If you have any most or less type of symptoms please do PCR test nearby and avoid communicating with other people.

Chain of infection

Covid-19 is highly infectious because of how easily for it to transmit with medium that you rarely realize. Here is 6 steps in the chain of infection

  • Infectious Agent: microorganism (in this case, a covid virus).
  • Reservoir: a host to allow for microorganism to grow and multiply.
  • Portal of Exit: a way for the microorganism to exit or escape from the host. Example: from the mouth, nose, blood, etc.
  • Mode of Transmission: Transportation for the microorganism transmitted from one to another. It can be from droplets, airbone, and direct or indirect contact.
  • Portal of Entry: a way for the microorganism to get into a new host. It can be from the mouth, nose, eyes, and any open wound/scars.
  • Susceptible Host: any host that have low immune condition. Example: a baby, elderly person, or anyone that have a sickness.

How to avoid getting covid and cuts chain of infection

During this covid-19 pandemic, there is many people that keep getting infected by covid-19, caused by lacking attention of how easy it is for covid-19 to infect someone. That is why we will put list here on how to reduce possibility of getting infected by covid-19.

Wear a mask correctly

Wearing a mask is one of the effective ways to cuts chain of infection. Wearing a mask will protect yourself and other people around you, because it blocks portal of exit and/or portal of entry, hence you will be most unlikely to get infected by covid and people around you can feel safe.

Clean your hands often

As we have mentioned before, covid-19 can be transmitted through indirect contact. That means anything of the infected people touch will also have covid-19 virus on it. That is why you should clean your hands often. You can buy hand sanitizer so you can clean your hands anytime, anywhere or you can wash your hands with soap and water which is more effective.
